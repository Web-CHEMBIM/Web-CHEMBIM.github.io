5.7 Properties of Ionic Compound and Covalent Compound

Difference between the formation of ionic bond and covalent bond

The difference between the ionic bonding and covalent bonding can be discussed in three aspects: how bonds are formed, types of particle and force between particles.

Ionic Bond Covalent Bond
Throught the transfer of electrons from metal atom to non-metal atom. How bonds are formed Through the sharing of electrons between non-metal atoms
Positively charged and negetively charged ions are formed Type of particles Molecules are formed
Strong electrostatic attraction between oppositely charged ions Force between particle Weak intermolecular forces of attraction between molecules
