Diagram below shows the dative bond in ammonium ion, NH4+.

1. What is dative bond?
Dative bond is a type of covalent bond where the electron pair that is shared comes from one atom only.
2. Described the formation of dative bond in ammonium ion, NH4+
The formation of ammonium ion, NH4+ occurs when ammonium reacts with hydrogen ion. Hydrogen ion, H+ does not have any electron in its shell. The nitrogen atom in ammonia has a pair of electrons that can be shared with the hydrogen ion. THe shared electrons form a dative bond.
